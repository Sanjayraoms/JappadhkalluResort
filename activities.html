Rope Activities

Nestled within the lush landscape, the resort offers an array of adventurous rope activities, including exhilarating challenges like the tyre walk, navigating an unstable bridge, and mastering the balance on a single rope, ensuring guests an unforgettable experience amidst nature's beauty.

Swimming Pools

The resort boasts two sprawling swimming pools complete with thrilling water slides, as well as convenient bathroom facilities, providing guests with endless fun and relaxation.



Event Hall

Set against breathtaking scenery, the resort features a generously sized event hall, providing ample space for a wide range of events and gatherings. Whether it's a wedding, conference, or special celebration, guests can enjoy the convenience of a well-appointed venue, designed to accommodate their needs while ensuring a memorable experience for all.


Group Activities

The resort offers an array of group activities, including cricket, badminton, and volleyball, providing guests with opportunities for friendly competition and camaraderie amidst the natural beauty of their surroundings. Whether guests are seeking to unwind with a leisurely match or engage in some spirited play, the resort's facilities cater to all levels of enthusiasm, ensuring an enjoyable stay for everyone.

Camp Fire

Set amidst the tranquility of nature, the resort provides an immersive experience with nightly campfires, where guests can unwind to the sound of crackling flames, accompanied by live music and a spacious dance floor, ensuring unforgettable evenings under the starlit sky.

Morning walk

Nestled in the heart of nature, the resort offers a serene morning experience with guided walks through picturesque tea estates, where guests can immerse themselves in the beauty of lush greenery. Additionally, the nearby river beckons, providing a tranquil setting for relaxation and exploration, ensuring a rejuvenating stay amidst natural splendor.

Rain dance

Amidst the backdrop of verdant landscapes, the resort beckons guests to revel in the exhilarating atmosphere of a rain dance. Here, beneath a canopy of sky and trees, visitors can surrender to the rhythm of music while cascading water showers down, creating an ambiance of lively celebration and carefree enjoyment amidst nature's embrace.
