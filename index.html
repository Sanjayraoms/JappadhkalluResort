Welcome to JappadhKallu Homestay

Jappadh kallu is a premier Home Stay amidst the lush greenery of Western Ghats in Hassan District of karnataka. Situated at a distance of 250 kms from bangalore and 150 kms from Mysore, its a perfect heaven or a weekend getaway from the hustle bustle of city life.

Rejuvenate your senses with the peaceful and serene surroundings of jappadhkallu homestay. Jappadhkallu is surrounded by evergreen forest, paddy fields, streacms, etc giving a marvelous view. Jappadhkallu Home Stay provides you with clean and well furnished rooms, large area for various activites around the homestay to make your experience a memorable one.

@ Jappadhkallu you can hear the chirping of exotic birds, smell the fragrance of wild flowers, taste locally grown coffee, go for a nature or plantation walk,....
